Purpose

I intend to make an app to make co-parenting easier. In many situations, a calendar is needed so the parents and the children can feel stable and avoid conflicts. Schedules get hectic with holidays, state guidelines, one-off scenarios, and step-parents in the mix. For example, my schedule is I have my son every Sunday, Monday, Every other Tuesday, and Every other Friday & Saturday. Can you tell me if I have my son 5 Tuesdays from now? Not so simple. Many divorce situations can even have additional step-parent splits with time. As of right now these types of schedules can be hard to track and can cause additional contention if misunderstood. I am making an app so all considerations can be put into one easy-to-use application and it will spit out a calendar.

Audience

Mostly for divorce situations that have splits of time for their children. This could also be used for other things but my app is geared toward that population.

Data Sources
